Who we are

Divine Fitness and Health Services, Inc. is a local health promotions organization in Baltimore, Maryland that creates programs that enhance the lives of all participants since 2003.

Divine Fitness has been creating innovative community-based physical fitness and health education programs since 2003. The company is owned and operated by Mrs. Tina Brown, who has a Master’s Degree in Health Sciences from George Washington University with a concentration in Clinical Research Administration (2007), specializing in Health Promotion, Chronic Disease Management, and Health Disparities/Health Equity in Minorities which is the basis for our business ventures. Mrs. Brown also holds various fitness certifications including Health Coaching, Personal Fitness Training, Kids and Teens Fitness; Older Adult Fitness. We are dedicated to meeting and exceeding all your health and fitness needs!
